Sleeping Habits

Getting enough sleep is just as important as eating well and staying active. Quality sleep helps improve focus, mood, and overall health, making it easier to handle daily challenges. If you often feel tired, struggle to concentrate, or wake up feeling unrested, it might be time to improve your sleep habits. Tips for better sleep; stick to a schedule, limit screen time, avoid caffeine late in the day, create a bedtime routine, make your sleep space comfortable. These options help you relax and give you a good night rest!
